Welcome to Hunter x Hunter

The best site to watch Hunter x Hunter anime ever!

Hunters devote themselves to dangerous tasks, such as traversing uncharted regions of the world and identifying rare objects and monsters. Before becoming a Hunter, one must pass the Hunter Exam, a high-stakes selection process in which most applicants end up disabled or, worse, dead. The participants who brave this famous exam have their own reasons for doing so. This is what drives 12-year-old Gon Freecss to search for his father, Ging, a Hunter. Believing he will meet his father when he becomes a Hunter, Gon takes the first step toward following in his footsteps. During the exam, Gon befriends Leorio Paladin, a medical student; Kurapika, a vengeful man; and Killua Zoldyck, a former assassin. Although their motivations are very different, they unite for a common goal and embark on an adventure in a dangerous world together.
